Shipping Information

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days (Monday-Friday, excluding public holidays).

Shipping Methods & Costs (Uganda)

Shipping costs are calculated based on your selected delivery location and the total weight/size of your order. The final shipping fee will be displayed during the checkout process after you select your location on the Cart Page.

  • Standard Delivery (Estimated 3-7 business days): Costs vary by district. Examples: Kampala area (~UGX 5,000-7,000), Major Upcountry Towns (~UGX 10,000-25,000), Remote Areas (~UGX 25,000+).
  • Express Delivery (Estimated 1-3 business days - Select Areas): Higher cost, available for certain locations. Option will appear at checkout if available for your address.
  • Pickup Stations: Choose a convenient pickup point in major towns during checkout. This is often a more affordable option (e.g., ~UGX 3,000-7,000). You will be notified when your order is ready for collection.

Promotions: We frequently offer free or discounted shipping for orders exceeding a certain value - check the top bar of our website or the cart page for current offers!

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email and/or SMS with tracking information, allowing you to monitor your delivery's progress.

International Shipping

Currently, we primarily ship within Uganda. For international shipping inquiries, please contact our customer support team via the Contact Us page to check feasibility and receive a quote.
