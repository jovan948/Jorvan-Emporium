Return Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, you can return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a refund or exchange, subject to the following conditions.

Conditions for Return

  • Items must be in their original condition: unworn, unwashed, undamaged.
  • All original tags, labels, and packaging (e.g., shoe boxes) must be intact and included.
  • Beauty, grooming, and personal care items must be unopened, unused, and in their original sealed packaging.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items generally cannot be returned unless faulty:

  • Final sale or clearance items clearly marked as non-returnable.
  • Bodysuits, lingerie, swimwear, underwear.
  • Earrings and other pierced jewelry.
  • Opened or used beauty, grooming, or personal care products.
  • Gift cards.
  • Items damaged after delivery.

How to Initiate a Return

1. Login: Access your Radiance Vault account and navigate to "My Orders".

2. Select Order & Items: Find the relevant order and click "Return Item(s)". Select the product(s) you wish to return and the reason.

3. Choose Return Method: Select your preferred return method (e.g., drop-off at a designated point, courier pickup - availability may vary).

4. Follow Instructions: Follow the on-screen instructions carefully. You may need to print a return label.

If you checked out as a guest or encounter issues, please contact our Customer Support team via the Contact Us page with your order details.

Refunds & Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item(s) (typically within 5 business days of receipt), we will process your refund.

  • Refunds are usually issued to the original payment method.
  • Store credit may be offered as an alternative.
  • Please allow an additional 3-7 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.
  • Original shipping fees are generally non-refundable, unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., wrong or faulty item sent).
