Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Browse products via categories or search. Click an item, select options (size/color), and click "Add to Cart". Go to the Cart Page and follow the steps for delivery and payment.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Contact customer support immediately via the Contact Us page. Modifications/cancellations depend on order status.

How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email upon shipment. You can also track via your account order history.

Shipping

What are your shipping options and costs?

Costs vary by location and are shown at checkout on the Cart Page. We offer standard delivery and pickup stations. See Shipping Info.

How long does shipping take?

Estimated times: Standard (3-7 business days), Express (1-3 days, select areas). See Shipping Info.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

Most items can be returned within 30 days if unworn/unused with tags. Exceptions apply (e.g., final sale, swimwear). See Return Policy.

How do I make a return?

Initiate returns via your account order history or contact support. See Return Policy.

Account & Payment

Do I need an account to order?

Guest checkout may be available, but an account helps track orders and manage wishlists.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel), Visa/Mastercard, and PayPal. See Payment Methods.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use secure encryption and trusted payment gateways.
